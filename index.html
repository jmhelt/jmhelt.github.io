About

I am a Senior Software Engineer at Databricks, working on Delta Live Tables and Enzyme, a system for incremental view maintenance in data engineering pipelines. Previously, I was a PhD student in Computer Science at Princeton University, advised by Professor Wyatt Lloyd. My dissertation research focused on consistency models for distributed storage systems.

Before my PhD, I earned an MS in Computer Science from Carnegie Mellon University and worked as a software engineer at Squarespace and Goldman Sachs.
