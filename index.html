Hello! I’m a PhD student in Computer Science at Princeton University advised by Professor Wyatt Lloyd. My interests are in distributed systems and networking, and my current research focuses on distributed databases.

Before attending Princeton, I completed a master’s at Carnegie Mellon University, where I was fortunate to be co-advised by Professors Srini Seshan and Vyas Sekar. I also spent a few years in industry, and before that, I earned my BA from Amherst College, where I was a member of the Amherst Men’s Swim Team.
