About

I am a Software Engineer at Databricks, working on Enzyme, a system for incremental view maintenance. Previously, I was a PhD student in Computer Science at Princeton University, advised by Professor Wyatt Lloyd. My dissertation research focused on consistency models and concurrency control for distributed databases.

Before my PhD, I earned an MS in Computer Science from Carnegie Mellon University and worked as a software engineer at Squarespace and Goldman Sachs.
